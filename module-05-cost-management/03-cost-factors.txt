Azure Cost Factors
------------------
Several factors influence the cost of Azure services.

Key cost drivers
- Resource type (VMs, storage, databases)
- Region (prices vary globally)
- Data transfer (egress costs)
- Storage redundancy (LRS, ZRS, GRS)
- Reservations (1-year or 3-year discounts)
- Spot pricing (unused capacity at deep discounts)

Examples
- Running a VM in East US may cost less than in Japan East
- GRS storage costs more than LRS due to geo-replication

Why it matters
Understanding cost factors helps optimize cloud spending.
