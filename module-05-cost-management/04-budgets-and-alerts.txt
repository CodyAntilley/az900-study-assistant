Budgets and Alerts
------------------
Azure Cost Management allows you to set budgets and receive alerts when spending approaches limits.

Budgets
- Define monthly, quarterly, or annual spending limits
- Track actual vs. forecasted costs

Alerts
- Trigger notifications at thresholds (e.g., 80%, 90%, 100%)
- Help prevent cost overruns
- Can notify individuals or groups

Use cases
- Preventing unexpected charges
- Monitoring team or project spending
- Enforcing financial governance

Why it matters
Budgets and alerts help maintain control over cloud costs.
