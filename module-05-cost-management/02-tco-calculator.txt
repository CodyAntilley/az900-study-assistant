Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) Calculator
----------------------------------------
The TCO Calculator compares the cost of running workloads on-premises vs. in Azure.

What it includes
- Hardware costs
- Software licensing
- Electricity and cooling
- IT labor
- Datacenter space

Benefits
- Shows long-term savings of cloud migration
- Helps justify cloud adoption to leadership
- Highlights operational efficiencies

Why it matters
Organizations use TCO to understand the financial impact of moving to Azure.
