Azure Pricing Calculator
------------------------
The Azure Pricing Calculator helps estimate the cost of Azure services before deployment.

What it does
- Lets you configure services (VM size, storage type, bandwidth)
- Provides monthly cost estimates
- Allows comparison of different configurations
- Helps plan budgets before provisioning resources

Use cases
- Estimating VM costs
- Comparing storage redundancy options
- Forecasting monthly cloud spend

Why it matters
It prevents unexpected costs by allowing you to model pricing ahead of time.
