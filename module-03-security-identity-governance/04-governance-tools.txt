Azure Governance Tools
----------------------
Azure Policy
Enforces rules and compliance across resources.
Examples:
- Allowed locations
- Enforce tagging
- Restrict VM sizes

Resource Locks
Prevent accidental deletion or modification.
Types:
- ReadOnly
- Delete

Management Groups
Organize multiple subscriptions into a hierarchy for governance.

Blueprints (Legacy)
Package policies, RBAC, and templates for consistent deployments.

Tags
Key-value pairs used for organization, cost tracking, and automation.

Why governance matters
Ensures consistency, compliance, and control across large environments.
