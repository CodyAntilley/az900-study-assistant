Azure Active Directory (Azure AD / Entra ID)
--------------------------------------------
Azure AD is Microsoft’s cloud-based identity and access management service.

What it provides
- Authentication (sign-in)
- Authorization (permissions)
- Single Sign-On (SSO)
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Conditional Access
- Identity protection

Key concepts
- Users: People or service accounts
- Groups: Collections of users for easier access management
- Service Principals: Identities for apps and automation
- Tenants: The Azure AD instance for your organization

Why it matters
Identity is the foundation of cloud security. Azure AD controls who can access what.
