Azure Advisor
-------------
Azure Advisor provides personalized recommendations to optimize your Azure environment.

Recommendation categories
- Cost: Reduce unnecessary spending
- Security: Improve security posture
- Reliability: Increase availability
- Performance: Improve speed and efficiency
- Operational Excellence: Best practices for management

Examples
- Resize or shut down underutilized VMs
- Enable MFA for admin accounts
- Add redundancy for critical workloads

Why it matters
Azure Advisor helps you follow best practices and optimize your cloud environment.
