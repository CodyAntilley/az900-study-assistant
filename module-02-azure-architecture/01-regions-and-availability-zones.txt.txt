Regions and Availability Zones
------------------------------
Regions
A region is a geographic location containing one or more Azure datacenters. Examples: East US, West Europe, Southeast Asia.

Why regions matter
- Performance (closer to users)
- Compliance (data residency)
- Service availability
- Pricing differences

Availability Zones
Physically separate datacenters within a region. Each zone has independent power, cooling, and networking.

Why zones matter
- High availability
- Protection from datacenter failures
- Zone-redundant services (ZRS)

Example
East US has multiple availability zones for redundancy.
